Unit 3 Grammar Presentation

Simple Present

Learn how to talk about care and prevention actions in English using the simple present. This presentation includes affirmative, negative, and question forms with examples and exercises.

Vocabulary focus

recycle, reuse, save water, plant trees, clean, protect, help, prevent

Main goal

Describe habits, actions, and recommendations for the community.

Part 1

What is the simple present?

We use the simple present to talk about routines, habits, actions, and general facts.

We recycle paper at school.
Students save water in the community.
People plant trees every year.
Part 2

Affirmative sentences

Subject + base verb + complement
He / She / It + verb-s / verb-es + complement
SubjectExample
I / You / We / TheyWe recycle bottles.
He / She / ItShe protects the river. / He helps the community.
The school reuses paper.
We clean the park every week.
Part 3

Negative sentences

Subject + do not (don't) + base verb
He / She / It + does not (doesn't) + base verb
We do not waste water.
They do not burn trash.
She does not litter in the street.
He does not pollute the river.
Part 4

Questions

Do + subject + base verb?
Does + he / she / it + base verb?
Do you recycle plastic?
Do they save water at home?
Does your community plant trees?
Does she protect the environment?
Part 5

Third person singular

With he, she, it, add -s or -es to the verb in affirmative sentences.

Add -s

help → helps

protect → protects

plant → plants

Add -es

go → goes

watch → watches

do → does (in questions/negatives)

Exercise 1

Choose the correct answer

1. She ___ the environment.
2. We ___ water at school.
Exercise 2

Complete the sentences

1. ______ you recycle at home?
2. He ______ waste water.
3. We ______ trees in our community.
Exercise 3

Change to negative

1. We recycle paper. → We do not recycle paper.

2. She protects the forest. → She does not protect the forest.

3. They clean the park. → They do not clean the park.

Final practice

Write your own sentences

  • Write 2 affirmative sentences with the simple present.
  • Write 1 negative sentence.
  • Write 1 question about care or prevention actions.
Example: We recycle bottles. My community saves water. Do people protect the river?

Excellent! Now you can talk about actions and habits in English.
