Unidad 4

Imperatives and Full Course Review

This presentation reviews the whole course from Unit 1 to Unit 4 and focuses on imperatives, community actions, vocabulary, and simple messages for products and services.

Focus

Give advice, instructions, and suggestions using imperatives.

Course review

Business, community problems, products, services, and promotional language.

Part 1

What are imperatives?

We use imperatives to give instructions, advice, warnings, or suggestions.

Verb + complement
Recycle paper.
Save water.
Plant trees.
Clean the street.
Part 2

Imperatives for community actions

Positive imperatives

Use them to encourage good actions.

Help your community.

Protect the river.

Reuse bottles.

Negative imperatives

Use Don't + verb to stop bad actions.

Don't litter.

Don't waste water.

Don't burn trash.
